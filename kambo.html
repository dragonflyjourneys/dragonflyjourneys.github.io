With gentle grace, the shaman purifies each participant, cleansing their energetic fields and preparing them to receive Kambo's potent medicine. The golden-green secretion of the Phyllomedusa bicolor frog, Kambo, is the medium through which the spirit of the forest imparts its profound healing touch.

Participants approach this sacred substance with humility and surrender, knowing that it holds the key to unlocking inner barriers and revealing the deepest aspects of their being. As the Kambo is lovingly applied to the skin, a sense of interconnectedness with the living world envelops each individual. A purifying fire ignites within, burning away stagnant energies and opening the channels of transformation.

The Kambo journey is a rite of passage, traversing the realms of mind, body, and soul. As the physical body experiences purgative sensations, the spirit embarks on an odyssey of self-awareness. Visions, insights, and revelations may arise, unfurling like vivid tapestries, inviting participants to confront their shadows and embrace their inner light.

In the sacred space of the Kambo ceremony, time loses its grip, and the veil between the tangible and intangible becomes translucent. Participants become attuned to the rhythms of the universe, sensing their place in the cosmic symphony. The sacred frog, guardian of the Amazonian wisdom, whispers its teachings, reminding us of our inherent connection to all life forms.

As the ceremony nears its conclusion, a profound sense of gratitude permeates the air, honoring the gifts of healing and self-discovery received. Participants emerge like butterflies from their chrysalis, reborn into a renewed sense of purpose and belonging.
