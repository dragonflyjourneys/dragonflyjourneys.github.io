Kambo Healing Sessions

Are you looking for a natural and traditional way to boost your physical and emotional wellbeing?

Kambo, also known as the "frog medicine," is a powerful Amazonian healing ritual that involves the application of a secretion from the skin of the giant green monkey frog (Phyllomedusa bicolor) to the skin. While the process may sound intense, many individuals who have experienced kambo report profound physical, emotional, and spiritual benefits.

Here are just a few of the potential benefits of kambo:

  • Physical healing: Kambo is known for its potent detoxifying effects, helping to purge the body of toxins and improve the function of the immune system. It has also been shown to have analgesic (pain-relieving) properties and can be helpful in treating chronic pain conditions.
  • Emotional healing: Kambo is often used to treat anxiety, depression, and other emotional imbalances. It can help individuals to release emotional blocks and traumas, leading to greater emotional balance and well-being.
  • Spiritual growth: Kambo is also used as a tool for spiritual growth and personal transformation. Many individuals who participate in kambo ceremonies report deep spiritual experiences, including a greater sense of connectedness to the universe and a heightened sense of intuition.
  • Increased energy and vitality: Following a kambo ceremony, many individuals report feeling more energized, focused, and motivated, with greater physical and mental endurance.

It's important to note that kambo should only be administered by a trained practitioner in a safe and supportive setting. While the process can be intense, many individuals who have experienced kambo report that it has been a life-changing and transformative experience, leading to greater physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.
