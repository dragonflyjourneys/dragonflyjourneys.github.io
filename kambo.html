Kambo Healing Sessions

Are you looking for a natural and traditional way to boost your physical and emotional wellbeing?

Kambo healing, also known as "frog medicine," is a traditional Amazonian practice that has been used for centuries by indigenous tribes for its powerful medicinal properties. It involves the application of the secretions of the Phyllomedusa bicolor frog onto the skin, creating a series of physical and psychological effects that are said to promote healing and spiritual growth.

The Kambo ceremony typically begins with the participant fasting for several hours before the application of the frog secretion. The practitioner heats the small wooden sticks, called "points," and applies them to the skin to create small burns. The frog secretion is then placed on the burns, and the participant begins to feel the effects of the medicine.

Kambo is known for its strong purgative properties, causing the body to expel toxins and impurities. It is also believed to stimulate the immune system, improve mental clarity, and relieve physical pain. Participants often report feeling a deep sense of relaxation and mental clarity after the ceremony, as well as improved physical health.

The frog secretion used in Kambo healing contains a unique combination of peptides that are not found in any other substance. These peptides have been shown to have a wide range of therapeutic effects, including pain relief, improved immune function, and reduced inflammation. The peptides in Kambo have also been studied for their potential use in treating conditions such as Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, and cancer.

Kambo is a powerful tool for physical and spiritual healing, but it should only be administered by a trained practitioner. The ceremony should be conducted in a safe and controlled environment, and participants should be screened for any underlying health conditions.
