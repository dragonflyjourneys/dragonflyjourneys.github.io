Holistic Education for Psychedelic Guides and Journeyers


This opportunity is aimed at experienced facilitators to refine their skills, challenge their perspectives, and deepen their understanding of the complex dynamics involved in facilitating profound subjective experiences, but everybody is welcome who would like to learn about the healing power of plants and mushrooms.

Drawing from a decade experience with entheogenic plants and extensive self exploration using diverse medicines and numerous profound experiences, often involving heroic doses of magic mushrooms, I have developed an understanding of the profound and transformative potential of these remarkable substances.

Through my extensive experience, I have witnessed firsthand the capacity of psychedelics to induce states of heightened self awareness, profound introspection, and a profound sense of interconnectedness with the natural world. These experiences have been instrumental in my own personal growth and spiritual development, allowing me to transcend the limitations of my ego and gain a more holistic perspective on the human experience.


Two-Week Training Modules: Exploring Consciousness and Psychedelic Journeys

Each module consists of one session per week, lasting for two weeks. Each session is designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical applications.
The cost of this service is 300 USD per module.
Additional sessions on request

Module 1: Understanding and Overcoming Limiting Beliefs

  • Maintaining an Open Mindset:
    • Explore the nature of consciousness through personal experiences, similar to shamanic practices.
    • Recognize the power of shaping your reality, field, and aura through vibrations.
    • Investigate the concept of the soul and its dual aspects (self and higher self).
  • Transcending Realms:
    • Learn about the potential to transcend physical, astral, and dream realms.
    • Study the present moment as a container of both past and future.
  • Cosmic Influences:
    • Examine the influence of the Sun, Moon, planets, and Earth on our lives.
    • Explore the dance of light and darkness and its impact on our existence.

Module 2: Diverse Spiritual Traditions and Shamanic Practices

  • Historical and Cultural Overview:
    • Trace the origins of shamanism and yogic practices.
    • Study the use of sacred plants and spiritual entities across cultures.
  • Interconnected Philosophies:
    • Understand the intersection of shamanic practices with Hinduism, Buddhism, and Ancient Egyptian thought.
    • Explore traditional knowledge systems such as kambo, ayahuasca, and Peganum harmala.
  • Healing Through Harmony:
    • Investigate how ancient traditions address illness and promote healing through harmonizing elements.

Module 3: Emotional Awareness and Sensitivity

  • Embracing Sensitivity:
    • Learn to manage and enhance emotional receptivity.
    • Create a safe space for emotional exploration and self-awareness.
  • Safety and Security:
    • Maintain wellbeing by surrounding yourself with positivity and uplifting experiences.
    • Foster inner peace and harmony through careful selection of environments and individuals.
  • Celestial Guidance:
    • Study how celestial bodies influence decision-making.
    • Align personal plans with natural rhythms for better outcomes.

Module 4: Preparing for a Psychedelic Journey

  • Personalization and Ceremony:
    • Embrace the uniqueness of each individual's experience.
    • Approach the journey with a mindset of ceremony and ritual.
  • Journey Dynamics:
    • Establish effective communication for navigating psychedelic journeys.
    • Build trust, respect, and openness to foster collaboration and creativity.
    • Explore the collective consciousness and gain insights into cosmic and personal connections.
  • Managing Experiences:
    • Investigate dosage levels and their effects.
    • Manage physical, emotional, and psychological responses.
    • Embrace experiences with an open mindset and address resistance.
  • Observing Patterns:
    • Observe unique patterns, sounds, and visuals.
    • Heighten the senses and reconnect with primal experiences.

Module 5: Fine-Tuning Psychedelic Journeys

  • Sensory Enhancement:
    • Utilize sound and light to create an immersive atmosphere.
    • Embrace rhythmic breathing, movement, and dance.
  • Authentic Expression:
    • Engage in singing and use of singing bowls.
    • Foster self-discovery through authentic communication and poetic expression.
  • Grounding Techniques:
    • Learn techniques to establish stability and connection.
    • Find moments of calm and rootedness during and after the journey.

Module 6: Psychedelic Integration

  • Holistic Approach:
    • Understand the transformative nature of psychedelic experiences.
    • Integrate lessons into everyday life to enrich existence.
  • Expanded Perspective:
    • Embrace expanded perspectives and allow insights to permeate reality.
    • Dissolve boundaries between the extraordinary and the mundane.
  • Approach of Maria Sabina:
    • Study the teachings and practices of Maria Sabina.

Module 7: Synergistic Combinations for Psilocybin Journeys

  • Enhancing Effects:
    • Learn how fasting and plant-based diets enhance psilocybin effects.
  • Supplementation:
    • Understand the modulation of experiences with Syrian Rue, Passionflower, or Caapi vine.
  • Influencing the Journey:
    • Explore the impact of incorporating tobacco, cacao, or THC.
  • Microdosing Practices:
    • Study the benefits of microdosing mescaline for stimulation and grounding.
  • Fragrance Use:
    • Learn how fragrances like frankincense, sage, and lemongrass provide additional insights.
