Syrian Rue - Peganum Harmala and Psylohuasca

The Syrian Rue, scientifically named Peganum Harmala, is often referred to as the “Ayahuasca" of North Africa and Eurasia. This plant shares certain characteristics with the renowned Amazonian ayahuasca. Traditionally, it has been utilized by healers to address mental health concerns, offering a distinctive synergy that contributes to comprehensive treatment approaches. Users often report longer lasting positive effects following the experience with this plant.
