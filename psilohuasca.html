🌌 Embrace the Wisdom of Nature:

In the heart of Psilohuasca lies the harmonious partnership of two natural gifts. Syrian Rue (Peganum harmala) has been honored for centuries for its therapeutic properties, revered as a teacher plant by indigenous cultures. Its MAO-inhibiting qualities synergize with Psilocybin, a compound found in select mushrooms, to create a bridge to altered states of consciousness and profound insights.
