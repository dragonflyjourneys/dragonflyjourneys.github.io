About the Dragonfly

The Dragonfly's transformation began approximately 15 years ago, fueled by an insatiable curiosity for the mysteries of the world. It all started when he consumed the sacred morning glory seeds (ololiuqui), revered by the Aztec and Maya cultures. This initial encounter sparked a deep fascination with the enchanting realm of magical plants and fungi.

Over the years, the Dragonfly gradually embraced these mystical tools, seeking a profound connection with each one. He nurtured a bond with sacred mushrooms by cultivating and regularly ingesting them. Drawn to the sacred geometry of cacti, he dedicated time to communing with the spirit of Wachuma (San Pedro), receiving guidance that led him to further explore the healing powers of the sacred Ayahuasca, the grandmother of the jungle.

Through years of experimentation with various plant spirits and recipes, the Dragonfly delved deeper into the realm of healing, embarking on a personal journey of self-discovery. It was during this time that the Ayahuasca grandmother guided him back to the sacred mushrooms, teaching him how to delve into the depths of his consciousness.

This revelation inspired the Dragonfly to combine the healing properties of the Syrian Rue (Peganum Harmala) plant with heroic doses of magic mushrooms. What followed was a period of profound self-discovery and learning, as he embarked on intense journeys of introspection. Along this transformative path, he accumulated experiences and knowledge, which gradually transformed into wisdom.

Now, having emerged from his cocoon, the Dragonfly unfurls his wings, ready to share the wisdom and magic he has acquired. He embodies the essence of transformation, offering guidance and inspiration to those who seek their own path of self-discovery and healing.
